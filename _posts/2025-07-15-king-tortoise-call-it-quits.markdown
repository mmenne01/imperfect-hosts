Local rock band “King Tortoise and the Hares” is calling it quits.  Lead Singer King Tortoise spoke out regarding musical differences between him and the Band; “I can’t play with musicians and a drummer that rush every song”.  On a side note, local band “The Sloths” said that they would be interested in working with Singer King Tortoise.
